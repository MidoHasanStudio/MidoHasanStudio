# Mido Hasan

Strategic brand and web design for founders ready to look as valuable as the work they do.

I build brand systems, websites, online stores, and WordPress tools that help founders close the gap between the value of their work and how their business is perceived online.

---

## What I build

**Brand strategy & identity systems**  
Clear positioning, visual direction, and identity systems that make a business easier to understand, remember, and trust.

**Websites that clarify value**  
High-quality websites built around structure, messaging, trust, and action — not decoration for the sake of decoration.

**Online stores built for buyer confidence**  
Storefronts designed to improve product perception, reduce hesitation, and make the buying decision feel clearer.

**WordPress products & systems**  
Themes, plugins, and admin workflows for creative businesses that need cleaner tools and better structure.

---

## How I think about design

People decide what your brand is worth before they understand what you offer.

That is why I care about:

- Clarity before visual noise
- Trust before trends
- Structure before decoration
- Perception before explanation
- Business value before design ego

A good website should not just look polished. It should make the offer easier to believe, easier to understand, and easier to act on.
